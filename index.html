01 Executive Profile

An enthusiastic AI researcher and academic leader with a Ph.D. in Informatics Engineering (Intelligent Systems) from the University of Coimbra, awarded summa cum laude (highest distinction). Currently serving as Dean of the Faculty of Engineering & Information Technology and Director of the AI & Innovation Center at Palestine Ahliya University, while maintaining an active research affiliation as Integrated Researcher at CISUC (Centre for Informatics and Systems, University of Coimbra).

Specializes in applied AI for content comprehension, biofeedback-augmented software engineering, large language model (LLM) systems, and agentic AI. Secured €20,000 in international research funding (February 2026) for the BRAIN project. Holds an international patent pending for an AI-driven biofeedback code review tool and has published in leading IEEE and Nature journals. Since 2023, serves annually as a Member of the DSN Doctoral Forum Committee (IEEE/IFIP International Conference on Dependable Systems and Networks) and as a peer reviewer for international journals and conferences. Combines deep technical expertise in modern LLM deployment, MLOps, and agentic workflows with a strong record in academic leadership, curriculum innovation, and interdisciplinary collaboration across Europe, the Middle East, and beyond.
